RUFARO

► DJ • Music Producer • Radio Personality • Youtuber • Nightlife Event Planner ◄

RUFARO is a creator. She creates & influences your mood as a DJ & music producer. She creates and provokes ideas, thoughts, and laughter as a Youtuber and radio show personality. As a nightlife event planner RUFARO turns an ordinary and mundane space into a mystical wonderland of whatever the hell she pleases.

This Zimbabwean American enchantress stepped into the nightlife scene in 2011. Since then she has founded and created HEAT & PUBLIC HARASSMENT. HEAT is an ever evolving space that promotes Tropical Bass. HEAT showcases Tropical Bass at nightclubs, radio stations, and on online social networks. As a result of HEAT’s popularity, RUFARO earned the nickname, “Moomba Queen” from local Boston DJ’s. Through HEAT, RUFARO has DJ’d alongside Kid Cedek, CODES, David Heartbreak, etc.

PUBLIC HARASSMENT is a radio show, youtube channel, and party that RUFARO created to promote dance music and the party lifestyle. As a radio show, RUFARO has DJ’d & interviewed many movers & shakers, celebrities, artists, & musicians. Some PUBLIC HARASSMENT interviewees include POSSO, CREEP STREET, Sam Baum of the Overthrow, JANE BANG, street artist & live painter Christina Angelina, and many many more.

As a DJ/producer RUFARO attributes her wide array of musical knowledge to travelling, culture hopping, and having been raised in the cultural melting pot of the U.S.A. RUFARO blends electro with the old and new sounds of house, nu disco, hip-hop, funk, world music, tropical bass, indie rock, etc. She does it all with an approachable underground flair & always maintains a sultry composure. A DJ set of RUFARO’s is always a pleasant adventure. You never know what you’re going to get but you’re always glad that you showed up for the ride.
